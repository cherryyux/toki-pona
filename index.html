tokipona
the language of good

toki pona is a minimalist constructed language created by Canadian linguist Sonja Lang in 2001. It is designed to be as simple as possible — the entire language has only ~137 words.

The name means "good language" or "simple language". By reducing vocabulary to the bare essentials, toki pona encourages speakers to think about the fundamental nature of things and express complex ideas through simple building blocks.

It takes most people just a few weeks to learn. This deck will guide you through the core vocabulary, sentences and grammar — one card at a time.
